Covid Vaccines that are approved in India

  • Manufactured by Bharat biotech in collaboration with ICMR.
  • This vaccine is basically inactivated corona virus which is injected into your body to produce antibodies.
  • Efficacy rate is 81% which has been declared after its 3rd phase of trials.
  • This vaccine is approved in India for now but not accepted worldwide yet.
  • The Oxford-AstraZeneca vaccine is manufactured in India by the Serum Institute of India in Pune.
  • This vaccine is prepared from a weakened version of common cold virus (adenovirus) from chimpanzee.This virus is modified to look like corona virus. But it wouldn't cause any illness.
  • This vaccine requires 2 doses with a gap of 4 to 12 weeks .
  • International clinical trials revealed its efficacy upto 90%.
  • This is a Russian vaccine developed by Moscow's Gamaleya Institute.
  • It uses cold-type virus,which is made harmless to be used as a carrier to inject small fragment of Corona virus into the body.
  • After getting this vaccine our body starts of produce antibodies specially tailored to the Coronavirus.
  • The difference of this vaccine from the other counterparts is that this vaccine uses different type of vector in the second dosage, inorder to attain the better immune response than the usual vaccines which use similar dosage both the times.
  • But,in India it is not yet available. It is being manufactured locally by REDDY'S Laboratories.
  • This vaccine has efficacy upto 92%.
  • Dosages are taken 21 days apart.
